the project's mission is to declutter sharing, resharing and co-using opportunities. all things on our planet have their purpose, let's call them items. nobody should store unused items in expensive storage containers, when somebody's else would activly use them! Due to overproduction, overconsumpditon and the capitalistic way of buying things, there was no economic solution for commons plattforms and ideas. Now with the concept of sustaniable zeroline and decentralized technologoy things will change and sharing items will be as easy as your next social post.

Milestones so far

  • March Sprint 1/3/ - 31/3/2020
    Monthly Sprint Experiment. 31 nights ~ about 70h workload to bring the project to a first stage.
  • Rebranding & Domain 2019
    Brand research completed: fljota which is the old norwegian verb for floating is the perfect match this project. Former worktitles SevenThings and BoomerAPP died due to marketing reasons. fljota.network comes to life.
  • Podcast Brainstorming 2018
    Listen to this Podcast Episode to understand the fundamental ideas about the sharing app and it's need for new technology.
  • Waiting for the right technology 2017
    Rise of Blockchain Technology, Smart Contracts and decentralized Apps restarted the idea in several workshops and meetups.
  • Would it be possible... 2009-2016
    A long discussion with a former AWS, now Google friend started one night in summer of 2009 about the sharing paradigm - it ended tough on sunrise on a balcony in the black-forest: there was no actionable solution for a resharing plattform.
